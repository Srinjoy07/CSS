Core Concept:
     central concepts in CSS that outlines the designs and layout of elements on the web page
Components:
    consists of four main components-margin,border,padding and content
Margin:
    the scope outside the border,seperating the elemenst from others.
Border:
    the outline that encapsulates the padding and component.
Padding:
    the space between the border and the actual content,providing a buffer
Content:
    the innermost layer where text,images or other media are housed
